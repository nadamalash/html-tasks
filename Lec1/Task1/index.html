Heading 1

The h1 tag is used for the main title of the page. It signifies the most important heading and is usually unique on a webpage.

Heading 2

The h2 tag is used for primary sections under the main title. It organizes the content into logical sections.

Heading 3

The h3 tag is used for subsections under an h2 section, offering further structure to the document.

Heading 4

The h4 tag is used for smaller subsections, often as descriptive titles within a section or subsection.

Heading 5

The h5 tag is used for minor sections that provide additional detail or smaller groups of information.

Heading 6

The h6 tag is used for the least important headings, often in footnotes or very detailed sections of a document.
